These data were collected to study public opinion on video games, both commercial and non-commercial, in relation to learning processes. They were used for the master’s thesis "Video Games and Learning: The Eclectic Potential of the Video Game Medium", presented in the 2024-2025 academic year at Ecclesia Mater University (Vatican City).

The samples have not been translated to preserve the integrity of the originals.
